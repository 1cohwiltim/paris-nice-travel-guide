# Paris to Nice Road Trip Guide

**Date:** 2026-03-23

## Paris Landmarks (pre‑departure)
Paris is renowned for its iconic landmarks, which attract millions of visitors annually. Key attractions include:
- **Eiffel Tower** – 324‑meter iron lattice tower with panoramic views.
- **Louvre Museum** – Home to the Mona Lisa and over 380,000 objects.
- **Notre‑Dame Cathedral** – Gothic masterpiece, recently restored.
- **Arc de Triomphe** – Honors those who fought in the Revolutionary and Napoleonic wars.
- **Sacré‑Cœur Basilica** – White‑domed church on Montmartre hill.
- **Champs‑Élysées** – Luxury avenue from the Arc to Place de la Concorde.
- **Centre Pompidou** – Modern art museum with inside‑out design.
